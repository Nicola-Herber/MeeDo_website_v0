Team

We are building MeeDo for doers

We combine product, design, and community-building experience to make spontaneous social activities simple and safe.

Nicola Herber — CEO & Co‑founder

Nicola is pursuing a Master’s in Electronic Engineering in Zurich, specializing in embedded systems.

He’s passionate about sports—especially wingfoiling and sailing—and teaches sailing each year at the Circolo Velico Lago di Lugano.

A curious traveler who loves meeting new people, he spent two months in Australia to level up his English and completed a three‑month thesis exchange in Boston at a Harvard Medical School lab.

Above all, he loves doing activities with friends—exactly the kind of moments MeeDo makes easier.

Samuele Dei Cas — COO & Co‑founder

Samuele first trained as a Polymechanic (AFC) before choosing Sports Science at the University of Fribourg. He’s now completing his Master’s in Sports Science in Lausanne.

Extremely outgoing, he teaches snowboarding, thrives in community settings, and loves bringing people together to do activities with friends—perfectly aligned with MeeDo’s mission.

Nicola Herber

CEO & Co‑founder

Nicola is pursuing a Master’s in Electronic Engineering in Zurich, specializing in embedded systems.

He’s passionate about sports - especially wingfoiling and sailing - and teaches sailing each year at the Circolo Velico Lago di Lugano.

A curious traveler who loves meeting new people, he spent two months in Australia to level up his English and completed a three‑month thesis exchange in Boston at a Harvard Medical School lab.

Above all, he loves doing activities with friends - exactly the kind of moments MeeDo makes easier.

Samuele Dei Cas

COO & Co‑founder

Samuele, 24, lives in Ticino and began his journey as a polymechanic (AFC), before dedicating himself to Sports Science at the University of Fribourg. He is now completing a Master’s in Sports Performance at the University of Lausanne.

Sociable and full of energy, in his free time he teaches snowboarding and practices gymnastics. He feels at ease in community environments and loves creating opportunities to bring people together through shared activities with friends - a lifestyle that fully embodies MeeDo’s mission.

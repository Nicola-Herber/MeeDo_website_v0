Project overview

A social utility for spontaneous activities

MeeDo reduces the friction of finding people to do things with — solving loneliness and coordination fatigue by matching intent, time, and location.

The problem

People want to be more active but often lack companions at the right time and place. Group chats are noisy; scheduling apps are rigid; communities are fragmented.

The solution

A lightweight way to create and join activities in seconds, discover nearby options on a map, and connect safely through profiles and preferences.

Who it’s for

Students, young professionals, hobbyists, travelers — anyone who wants to do more, together.

What's next

Roadmap

Our plan to bring MeeDo to life and grow the community.

Phase 1

MVP Development

Building the core experience: create activity, join nearby, and chat. Initial testing with our closed beta group.

Phase 2

Launch

Public release on App Store. We'll gather initial feedback to refine the app and ensure people love it!

Phase 3

Early Funding

Once validated our vision, we'll raise seed capital to fuel our growth. Every great project needs the right resources to become a unicorn.

Phase 4

Growth / Market Fit

Combining our roadmap with user feedback to ensure rapid growth and a product that truly satisfies our community.

Phase 5

Monetization

Don't worry ;) No paywalls. We're designing a win-win model that adds value for everyone without charging users to use MeeDo.

Phase 6

Series A

Scaling up operations, expanding the team, and taking MeeDo to international markets.

Phase 7

IPO

The ultimate goal: becoming a public company owned by the community that helped build it.

Under the hood

Technology

Built for performance and scale using modern, reliable tools.

React Native

We use React Native to deliver a truly native experience on both iOS and Android from a single codebase. This allows us to iterate fast and maintain feature parity across platforms.

Supabase

Our backend is powered by Supabase, providing us with a robust PostgreSQL database, real-time subscriptions for chat and live updates, and secure authentication out of the box.
